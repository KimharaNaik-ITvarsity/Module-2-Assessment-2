ABOUT THE STORE

Mini-Bowling, racing, bumping, shooting, hoops and so much more! The Fun Company offers the latest in family entertainment with attractions such as Mini Bowling, the latest Arcade Games, and Bumper Cars. All you need to do to get in on the action is buy a Fun Company play card in store (gone are the days of tokens and tickets), load your desired amount and swipe to activate the game. If you like to test your skills and the thrill of chasing tickets, then simply let the e-tickets accumulate to your play card and redeem them for a prize at the end of your visit or bank them to save up for a mega prize
