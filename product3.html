ABOUT THE STORE

Nike, Inc., is one of the largest and best-recognized global sports and athleticwear brands. Its extensive lineup includes its long-running Air Jordan, Air Force 1, and other “Air” models. Converse shoes and apparel (including those bearing the iconic Chuck Taylor All Stars logo) have also been a part of Nike since its 2003 acquisition.

Nike is known for its celebrity endorsement deals with top athletes, including Tiger Woods, LeBron James, and Serena Williams. The company has stayed relevant with consumers over the years through its savvy marketing, which includes embracing controversial topics. Since 2020, Nike has been led by president and CEO John Donahoe.
